Project Overview

Project Name: Art Showcase Webpage

Project Overview:

This application will primarily be a page where my client can show off their artwork. The users of this application are my client, who will show off their works, and interested patrons, who theoretically could order commission artworks if the site were fully functional. The primary focus will be on displaying the art, simulating paying for art, and sharing information on the artist.
